Pong is a two-player game. Each player controls a paddle on the screen by using the "Left" and "Right" arrow keys of the keyboard. The players' goal is to use their paddle to strike the ball back and forth.

The ball begins in the middle of the screen, and once the game starts, the ball will move in a random direction. If a player hits the ball, it bounces back towards the other player. Points are scored when one player fails to return the ball to the other player.

The game continues until one player reaches a score of 11 points. The player who first reaches 11 points is declared the winner. If the game is tied at 10 points, then play continues until one player has 2 points more than the other player, and that player is declared the winner.
